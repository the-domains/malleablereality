Rumour has it that there were once two brothers who didn’t get along. Like most siblings, Marco and Pedro would fight…a lot. In an attempt to stop the feuding, their father would take them to the family restaurant after school and put them to work. It was during these long summers that their father’s love of food finally rubbed off on them. And so, armed with a secret family recipe for orange shrimp, they eventually opened up rival restaurants of their own somewhere along the same stretch of beach.Or so the story goes, depending on who tells it…
